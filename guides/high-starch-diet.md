# High Starch Diet Guide

## Overview
A diet rich in complex carbohydrates designed to support energy needs and metabolic health.

## Key Principles
- Focus on complex, starchy carbohydrates
- Moderate protein intake
- Lower fat intake
- Regular meal timing

## Recommended Foods
### Primary Starch Sources
- Sweet potatoes
- White potatoes
- Brown rice
- Quinoa
- Oats
- Corn
- Winter squash

### Supporting Foods
- Lean proteins
- Vegetables
- Fruits
- Legumes

## Meal Structure
- 55-65% calories from carbs
- 15-20% from protein
- 20-30% from fats

## Best Practices
- Time starches around activity
- Choose whole grain versions
- Include fiber-rich foods
- Stay hydrated
